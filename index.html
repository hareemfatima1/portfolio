The Man Behind the Press

Printing the Future,
Honouring the Past

When art meets technology, innovation is born. This is the core philosophy behind the journey of Omer Nusrullah, known professionally as 'Omer N Raja.'

As a distinguished professional who has earned both national and international gold medals and honors in Institute for Printing and Graphic Arts, alongside a 1st Position from the National College of Arts (NCA), Omar combined his artistic vision with industrial expertise. Since joining Humayun Printers Private Limited in 1995, he has transformed it into a massive, multi-acre industrial hub that sets benchmarks in printing, packaging, and rigid boxes.

What truly sets him apart is his pioneering work on a global scale. By blending offset printing with corrugation, he introduced a level of international development and merchandising quality that was unheard of in the industry worldwide. Today, as the visionary behind Omer N Raja Studio, Ora Prints, BoxGhar, and the Humayun Trust, he continues to redefine packaging standards while sharing his wealth of knowledge through his industry-focused podcast. His life is a powerful narrative of passion, constant growth, and doing what others think is impossible.
