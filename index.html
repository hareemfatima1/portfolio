The Man Behind the Press

Printing the Future,
Honouring the Past

When art meets technology, innovation is born. This is the core philosophy behind the journey of Omer Nusrullah, known professionally as 'Omer N Raja.'

As a distinguished professional who has earned both national and international gold medals and honors in Institute for Printing and Graphic Arts, alongside a 1st Position from the National College of Arts (NCA), Omar combined his artistic vision with industrial expertise. Since joining Humayun Printers Private Limited in 1995, he has transformed it into a massive, multi-acre industrial hub that sets benchmarks in printing, packaging, and rigid boxes.

What truly sets him apart is his pioneering work on a global scale. By blending offset printing with corrugation, he introduced a level of international development and merchandising quality that was unheard of in the industry worldwide. Today, as the visionary behind Omer N Raja Studio, Ora Prints, BoxGhar, and the Humayun Trust, he continues to redefine packaging standards while sharing his wealth of knowledge through his industry-focused podcast. His life is a powerful narrative of passion, constant growth, and doing what others think is impossible.

What We Do

Printing Services

01

Commercial Offset Printing

High-volume, precision-colour offset printing using Heidelberg technology for brochures, catalogues, annual reports, magazines, and corporate collateral of the highest calibre.

Packaging & Label Design

Custom packaging solutions engineered for impact — from concept to shelf. Structural design, CMYK and spot colour, embossing, lamination, and die-cutting for premium brands.

Brand Publishing

Books, coffee-table publications, and limited-edition brand pieces that tell your story with the permanence only fine print can offer. Photography, editorial, and production under one roof.

Full-service creative studio offering brand identity, layout design, colour management, and pre-press production — ensuring your vision translates flawlessly onto paper.

05

Digital & On-Demand Print

Fast-turnaround digital printing for short-run projects without compromising quality. Variable data, personalisation, and on-demand fulfilment for modern marketing needs.

The Story

A Six-Decade Journey

1963
The Founding

Hamayuns Printers is established in Lahore, Pakistan, with a vision to bring quality commercial printing to the nation's growing business community.

1980s
Technology Adoption

The company invests in cutting-edge Heidelberg offset printing machinery, establishing its reputation as a technologically progressive firm in the Pakistani print industry.

2000s
ISO & HACCP Certification

Hamayuns Printers achieves ISO 9001-2008 and HACCP certifications, cementing its position as a quality-first enterprise trusted by international clients.

2016
Gold Sponsor — Pakistan Mega Leather Show

Hamayuns Printers is recognised as the Gold Sponsor of the Pakistan Mega Leather Show, demonstrating the brand's prestige within Pakistan's premier business and industrial events.

Today
Omer N. Raja at the Helm
